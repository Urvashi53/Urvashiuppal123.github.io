About Us

Feel 'O' Fly mart opened its gates for its customers on 16 th November 2010. It was initially a grocery store that slowly expanded up into a departmental store as well, through the love and faith of its customers. Today, Feel 'O' Fly mart is both a grocery as well as a departmental store that sells fresh grocery products such as fruits, veggies, dairy products, pulses, oil, etc. it also sells the items of a departmental store such as clothing, footwear, makeup, perfumes, skin care products, etc. It has evolved through the love of its customers by following the policies of love, care, and honesty and wishes to serve with the same for as long as possible.For any further query or information related to this mart, mail us at Feel 'O' Fly
